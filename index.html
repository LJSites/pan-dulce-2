Around the corner,
open early.

Pan Dulce #2 is the kind of panadería you walk to. Hyde Park Boulevard sidewalk, dogs in tow, a couple of dollars in your pocket. You leave with a small box, a cinnamon-sugar concha for the road, and the smell of fresh bread in your jacket.

Our regulars come for the tortas (especially the veggie one — order it without meat and thank us later), for our cakes and cookies that priced right for a Tuesday treat, and because the customer service makes you feel like you're at your tía's.
